FEFO. Shelf Life.

FEFO · First Expired First Out
Automatic Expiry-Based Stock Rotation
FEFO is enforced at the system level — every pick, transfer, and sale order automatically selects the nearest-expiry lot first. No manual intervention, no risk of expired stock reaching the customer. Configurable per product category and storage location.
Shelf Life · Expiry Management
Proactive Shelf Life Tracking
Every product assigned a shelf life at production — fresh chicken (days), frozen portions (weeks/months). System tracks remaining life per lot in real time, triggers configurable alerts before critical thresholds, and blocks dispatch of expired stock automatically.
  • Raw-to-finished cost transfer — costs roll forward at every transformation step automatically.

  • Sale & export management — customer orders, pricing by grade/category, dispatch, and invoicing.
  • Accounting integration — every production move creates the correct journal entry automatically.
